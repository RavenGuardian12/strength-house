About Dakota

Trained Hard.
Coached Right.

Dakota Moore built Strength House from the ground up — not to be another gym, but to be the training experience he wished existed. A private, distraction-free space where every session is intentional and every client actually gets results.

With over 6 years of coaching experience and 150+ clients transformed, Dakota specializes in strength training, body composition, and building sustainable habits that last. His approach is direct, science-backed, and built around you — not a template.

Whether you're picking up a barbell for the first time or chasing a new personal record, Dakota will meet you exactly where you are and take you further than you expected.
